1. About BinDash

BinDash ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is a residential bin-moving service operating in the Phoenix Metro Area, Arizona. We connect homeowners ("Homeowners") with local service providers ("Runners") who move trash and recycling bins to the curb before collection day and return them afterward.

BinDash operates through a web-based platform accessible at www.bindash.org and www.bindash.shop.

You are responsible for all activity that occurs under your account. BinDash reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that provide false information or violate these Terms.

3. Services Offered

BinDash offers the following service types:

Subscription Plans

  • 1-Can Plan — Weekly Bin Out & Bin In for one trash or recycling can.
  • 2-Can Same Day Plan — Weekly Bin Out & Bin In for two cans on the same collection day.
  • 2-Can Split Day Plan — Weekly service for two cans with different collection days (e.g., recycling Monday, trash Thursday).

Bin Out As Needed (OAN)

  • On-demand bin moving requested by the Homeowner when needed rather than on a fixed weekly schedule.
  • Available in 1-can, 2-can same day, and 2-can split tiers.
  • Includes a base monthly fee plus a per-visit charge.

À-La-Carte (Pay-Per-Visit)

  • Individual bin runs booked one at a time with no subscription required.
  • Charged per visit at the current posted rate.
  • Requires a valid payment method on file.

All services include both a Bin Out (moving bins to the curb the evening before collection) and a Bin In (returning bins after the truck passes) unless otherwise specified.

4. Scheduling & Service Windows

  • Bin Out runs are performed the evening before your scheduled collection day, typically between 6:00 PM and 9:00 PM.
  • Bin In runs are performed after your trash truck has passed, typically between 8:00 AM and 2:00 PM on collection day.
  • Exact times may vary based on runner availability, weather, or other operational factors.
  • BinDash will notify you via SMS or app notification when your bins have been moved.
  • Service is not guaranteed on officially recognized holidays. We will notify you in advance when holiday schedules apply.
Please note: BinDash is a best-effort service. While we strive for 100% completion, occasional delays or missed visits can occur due to runner availability or unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, we will contact you promptly and arrange a resolution.
