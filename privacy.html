1. Who We Are

BinDash ("BinDash," "we," "us," or "our") is a residential bin-moving service operating in the Phoenix Metro Area, Arizona. We connect homeowners with local service providers ("Runners") to handle curbside bin placement and retrieval around trash collection days.

Our platform is accessible at www.bindash.org and www.bindash.shop. For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@bindash.org.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways: directly from you, automatically when you use our platform, and from third-party service providers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

  • Providing the service — creating and managing your account, scheduling and dispatching bin runs, assigning Runners to routes.
  • Communications — sending service notifications, route completion alerts, schedule changes, and account updates via email and SMS.
  • Payment processing — billing for subscription plans and per-visit charges, processing Runner payouts.
  • Route optimization — using your geocoded property location to build efficient runner routes.
  • Safety — sharing necessary property access details (gate codes, pet info) with assigned Runners so they can complete service safely.
  • Platform improvement — analyzing usage patterns (in aggregate, never individually identifiable) to improve reliability and features.
  • Legal compliance — meeting obligations under applicable Arizona and federal law.

We do not use your information for advertising, behavioral profiling, or sale to data brokers.

4. SMS & Text Message Communications

SMS consent is separate from general account consent. By providing your mobile phone number during signup and checking the SMS consent box, you agree to receive text messages from BinDash for service-related notifications only.

What we text you about:

  • Confirmation that your bins have been moved to the curb.
  • Confirmation that your bins have been returned.
  • Route delays, schedule changes, or missed service alerts.
  • Emergency pickup confirmations.
  • Account and payment notifications.

We will never text you:

  • Promotional or marketing messages.
  • Third-party advertisements.
  • Messages unrelated to your BinDash service.

Message frequency:

Typically 2–4 messages per collection day, depending on your service type. Frequency varies.

How to opt out:

Reply STOP to any BinDash text message at any time. You will receive one final confirmation and no further messages. You can also manage SMS preferences in your account dashboard. Opting out of SMS does not cancel your BinDash service.

Help:

Reply HELP to any BinDash text or email support@bindash.org.

Msg & data rates may apply. SMS services are powered by Twilio. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure hosted in the United States. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our platform is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Database access is protected by row-level security (RLS) policies — each user can only access their own data.
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe and never stored on BinDash servers.
  • Gate codes and access information are stored encrypted and only surfaced to actively assigned Runners during a live route.
  • Admin access to the platform requires multi-factor authentication.
No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by law.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Active accounts — data is retained for the duration of your account plus 12 months after cancellation, to handle any billing disputes or service inquiries.
  • Job and route history — retained for 24 months for billing verification and service quality review, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Payment records — retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations.
  • SMS opt-out records — retained indefinitely to honor your preference and comply with TCPA regulations.
  • Deleted accounts — personal data is removed within 30 days of a deletion request, except where retention is legally required.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction — update inaccurate or incomplete information through your account dashboard or by contacting us.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated personal data. Some data may be retained as required by law.
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt out of SMS — reply STOP to any text message or update preferences in your dashboard.
  • Opt out of email notifications — unsubscribe via any email or update preferences in your dashboard.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@bindash.org with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

BinDash is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@bindash.org and we will promptly delete it.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know — you may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you in the past 12 months.
  • Right to Delete — you may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale — we do not sell personal information. No opt-out is needed, but you may contact us to confirm this.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising any CCPA rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email support@bindash.org with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days.

11. Cookies & Tracking

BinDash uses minimal tracking technology:

  • Session storage — used to maintain your login session while you use the app. Cleared when you close your browser.
  • Local storage — used to remember your preferences (such as which page you were viewing) between sessions. No personal data is stored here.
  • No third-party advertising cookies — we do not use Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising tracking.
  • No analytics trackers — we do not use Google Analytics or similar services that profile your behavior across websites.

You can clear local and session storage at any time through your browser settings. This will log you out of BinDash but will not affect your account data.
