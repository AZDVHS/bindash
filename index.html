What We Do

Comprehensive Home Watch
& Property Care

Every visit is QR-verified, photo-documented, and delivered to your inbox within the hour. We cover the entire Phoenix Metro Area with licensed, insured agents you can trust.

🔍
Home Watch Inspections
Interior and exterior walkthroughs using a custom checklist built specifically for your property. Every room, every visit — tailored to what matters most in your home.
📸
Photo Documentation
High-resolution photos of every area inspected, organized by room and archived to your client portal.
📍
QR-Verified Visits
Agents scan a unique QR code on arrival and departure — tamper-proof, instant, and works everywhere including indoors.
🏊
Pool & Spa Monitoring
Visual check of water clarity (clear, dirty, or green), chlorine and pH level reading, confirmation the pump and equipment are running, and safety fence verification. We do not service, treat, or repair pool equipment.
❄️
HVAC & Systems Check
We turn on the A/C and confirm it is cooling, visually inspect the exterior unit for obvious issues, and note the condition of accessible systems. Filter changes are available as an add-on service. Any additional HVAC work is coordinated with a licensed contractor at your direction.
🌿
Exterior & Landscape
Visual inspection of roof condition from ground level, gutters, driveway, and yard. We look for signs of irrigation issues (dead plants, flooded areas, wet spots) — not individual heads. We do not get on the roof or inspect every irrigation component. Monsoon damage is flagged immediately.
🚨
Emergency Dispatch
Storm damage or maintenance issues dispatched to vetted vendors on your behalf within 48 hours.
📱
Owner Dashboard — 24/7 Access
Review every inspection photo, track visit history, request services, and communicate with your team — anytime, from any device.
📋 Scope of Service — What to Expect Our visits are professional visual inspections. We document, photograph, and report what we observe. We do not perform mechanical repairs, treat pool water, clean gutters, or access rooftops. Filter changes and minor add-on services are available separately. Roof inspections are ground-level visual checks. Irrigation checks look for signs of failure — not individual head testing. If we find an issue, we document it with photos and alert you immediately — then you decide how to proceed.
