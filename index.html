How it works

Simple shifts, consistent pay, total flexibility

🌙
Evening — Bin Out
The night before collection day, move bins from properties to the curb. Routes are usually 6–10 properties, all within walking or cycling distance of each other. Takes about 45–60 minutes.
☀️
Morning — Bin In
After the waste truck has passed, return bins to properties. You'll get a push notification when your zone unlocks. Same stops as the night before — you already know the route.
📬
Offers come to you
BinDash sends batch offers 24 hours in advance. You have first refusal on your zone. Accept the whole batch at once — both visits, one tap. No bidding, no guessing.
💵
Weekly payout
Earnings are calculated automatically and paid weekly to your bank account. You're an independent 1099 contractor — set your own hours, no minimum commitment.
